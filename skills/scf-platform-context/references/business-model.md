# Business Model

- SCF is currently a small internal delivery team of roughly 6 people.
- The service population is international-school high school students applying to overseas universities.
- The current software users are internal SCF staff, not external SaaS customers.
- The website remains the mother port and the runtime business source of truth.
- The product's current value is internal execution efficiency and delivery clarity, not a generalized school-management platform.
- The current V2 expansion is about making the existing delivery loop easier to run through `SMS` reminders, sales handoff, and Tencent Meeting assisted feedback, not about replacing the website with a new front system.
- Product decisions should stay anchored to the actual delivery chain: intake, project design, teaching, scheduling, feedback, and internal coordination.
